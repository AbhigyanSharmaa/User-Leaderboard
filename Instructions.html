📘 Scoring System Guide

  • You get 100 points for signing up (which you’ve already received).
  • For each day you maintain your streak, you get: 5 points × current day number E.g., If it’s Day 4, you’ll get 5×4 = 20 points
  • Breaking a streak will cost you: Last streak length × 20 points So, if your streak was 5 days and you broke it, you lose 100 points (5×20) Your streak will reset to 0. Start again (but don’t start posting progress in Day 1’s post — just comment in the current day’s post).
  • Even 30 mins of study is absolutely okay. Please don’t quit.
  • To stay consistent, update your progress daily via comments in your study group or wherever your data is collected.
